## What We Offer

Create your stunning bachata profile and join the world's largest community of bachateros. Our platform gives you the tools to showcase your dance journey, connect with fellow dancers, and build your reputation in the bachata world.

## Key Features

- **Dynamic Profile Builder**: Create beautiful profiles with photos, videos, and detailed skill descriptions that tell your bachata story
- **Global Community**: Connect with dancers worldwide, follow your favorite teachers, and discover new dance partners across different cities and countries
- **Skill Level Tracking**: Get matched with appropriate classes and partners based on your skill level and preferred bachata style
- **Portfolio Showcase**: Upload performance videos, class demonstrations, and event photos to build your professional reputation
- **Teacher Verification**: Professional instructors can display credentials, certifications, and student testimonials to build trust

## How It Works

1. **Sign Up**: Create your account and choose your role (student, teacher, or both)
2. **Build Your Profile**: Add photos, videos, and details about your bachata journey
3. **Connect**: Follow other dancers, join communities, and discover new opportunities
4. **Grow**: Track your progress, get feedback, and advance your bachata skills
